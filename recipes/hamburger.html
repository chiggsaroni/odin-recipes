Hamburger

Recipe Title

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup cheese, shredded
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ounce of dry onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon worchestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Your favorite toppings

Directions

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Use your hands to form the mixture into 4 patties.
  2. Cook patties on the preheated grill or pan until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 to 5 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  3. Assemble the burger with your favorite toppings
